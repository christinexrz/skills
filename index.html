How it works

Set it up in minutes, run it anytime.

1

Onboard once

The first run asks a few questions (your channels, calendar, notes folder, repos, and goals) to customize and set up the skill. No config file to hand-edit.

2

Gather the week

Pulls the last 7 days of context from all the sources you set up and gives you a readable brief.

3

Analyze your effectiveness

Checks your week against your goals and the 3 Levels of Product Work (Execution, Strategy, Optics - from Shreyas), shows where your time went and recommends how you can be more effective.

4

One brief

Writes a 5-minute read and prepends it to the last one, so the file becomes a running memory of your quarter. Repurpose the content for your "optics" work such as leadership updates and self reviews.

Why every PM needs it

Learn from your busy days, not get burried by context switching

Assesses your effectiveness

It assesses your week to show you trends on how your time is spent and give actionable recommendations on things to watch.

A 5-minute read

When all the slack messages and meetings are consolidated in one place, you'll catch patterns and signals and sometimes even important decisions you've missed.

A running quarter memory

Every dump prepends to the last. You can run further analysis over months to extract more learnings or repurpose for self reviews.
